Search Info

The search filter will filter by item name, along with masterwork, mod and perk names. It also has a few special commands (some of which match DIM's filters)

  • AND let's you type in multiple filters at once. For example godroll AND rampage or even alloy AND rampage AND zen
  • OR let's you type in multiple filters at once. For example solar OR void (OR causes AND to be ignored, they cannot be combined)
  • ! adds a NOT filter to your query. !pulse monitor will show guns missing Pulse Monitor. !zen AND rampage will show guns with rampage but not zen moment.
  • is:godroll Show only items that match a god roll from a curated list of rolls (see the docs links on the top right for more info)
  • is:godrollpvp is:godrollpve Selected by PvE vs PvP god rolls
  • is:goodrollpvp is:goodrollpvp is:goodrollpve Rolls with all good perks, but not full god rolls
  • is:highest Shows the highest PL item for each slot (best to avoid sharding this if you only have one!)
  • is:cap<1360 Shows gear by its max power level. Supports >, <, >=, <=, and =
  • is:fixme Show items with godroll/goodroll perks that aren't currently active
  • is:masterwork Has a fully upgraded masterwork socket
  • is:hasmod Has a mod applied
  • is:random Is a random roll, and thus cannot be restored from Collections
  • is:fixed Is a fixed roll, you can always grab it from collections
  • is:light>=900 Finds gear by Light Level (aka Power Level). Supports >, <, >=, <=, and =
  • is:stattotal>=60 Finds armor by total stat points, same options as for is:light above
  • is:prefpoints>=30 Finds armor by total *preferred* stat points, same options as for is:light above
  • is:locked and is:unlockedFilters by lock state
  • season:worthy or season:undying/dawn/opulence/drifter/forge/outlawFilters by specific season on armor
  • has:arrivals or has:undying/dawn/opulence/drifter/forge/outlawShows all armor that can hold that season's mod slot (so Season of Worthy armor would show for any of Arrivals + Worthy + Dawn)
  • is:extratagged This shows cases where you have more than one item marked Upgrade/Keep for a given slot+energy (+frame for guns). Similar to the '+2/2' button under "Copies"

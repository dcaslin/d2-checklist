Donate @DestinyChecklst /r/destinychecklistnet Source Code

This site's goal is to help people have more fun with Destiny 2. As the game changes so will its features. Focus is generally on tracking data for yourself, your friends and your clan.

This site is open sourced on GitHub, and actively supported via Reddit and Twitter.

If you don't like ads, just pop over to the settings page and disable them. The site will remember your settings in the future. That same settings page also has options to choose a dark (or pink) theme.
